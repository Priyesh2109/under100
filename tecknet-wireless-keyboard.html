TECKNET 2.4G Wireless Mini Keyboard

Whisper-quiet scissor keys with 90% noise reduction and 12 months of battery life. Compact, wireless, and just works.

Why it made the list

Not everything on this site needs to be a statement piece. Sometimes the best buys are the boring ones — the things that quietly solve a problem and never give you grief. This TECKNET keyboard is exactly that. It's compact, it's wireless, it's almost completely silent, and it costs less than a round of drinks.

The scissor-switch keys give it a satisfying low-profile feel that's comparable to a decent laptop keyboard. It connects via a tiny USB receiver with zero setup — plug it in and it works. The 15-metre wireless range means you can use it from across the room, and the automatic sleep mode stretches battery life to around 12 months on two AAA batteries.

Who it's for

Anyone who needs a reliable, quiet keyboard without spending much. It's ideal for a home office setup, a secondary keyboard for the living room, or something compact to throw in a bag. If you work in a shared space or take calls while typing, the near-silent keys are a genuine advantage. At under £15 it's practically disposable — but it doesn't feel like it.

The verdict

It's hard to find fault at £14.99. The build quality is solid for the price, the typing experience is comfortable, and the wireless connection is reliable out to 15 metres. It's a keyboard that does its job well.
